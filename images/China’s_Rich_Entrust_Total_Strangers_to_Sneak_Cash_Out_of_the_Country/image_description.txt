The chart titled "Wealth Check" from the UBS Global Wealth Report 2023 displays the number of millionaires in selected countries. The data shows:

- The United States has the highest number of millionaires at 22.7 million.
- Mainland China follows with 6.2 million millionaires.
- France and Japan each have 2.8 million millionaires.
- Germany has 2.6 million millionaires.

Overall, the United States significantly leads in the number of millionaires, surpassing the combined total of the other listed countries.
