In the second quarter, India's stock market experienced significant growth, adding $440 billion to its market capitalization. The graph, sourced from Bloomberg and FT, tracks the market capitalization of India's stock market from the second quarter of 2022 (Q2 22) to the second quarter of 2023 (Q2 23) in trillions of US dollars.

Key highlights include:

- A general upward trend in market capitalization with fluctuations.
- A notable dip around Q4 22 and Q1 23.
- A sharp increase in market capitalization towards the end of Q2 23, reaching approximately $3.5 trillion.

This indicates a robust recovery and growth phase in India's stock market during this period.
