The chart displays the number of Chinese individuals residing in Japan with a business-management visa from 2017 to 2023. The yearly data are as of June each year, based on information from the Immigration Services Agency.

Here is a summary of the key points:
- In 2017, there were around 11,000 Chinese residents in Japan with business-management visas.
- The number saw a steady increase, reaching approximately 12,000 in 2018 and maintaining a similar level in 2019 and 2020.
- In 2021, the count slightly decreased but remained close to 12,000.
- There was a slight increase again in 2022, bringing the number to over 12,000.
- The most significant rise occurred in 2023, with the number jumping to around 17,000.

Overall, from 2017 to 2023, there has been a discernible increase in the number of Chinese individuals with business-management visas residing in Japan, with a notable spike in 2023.
