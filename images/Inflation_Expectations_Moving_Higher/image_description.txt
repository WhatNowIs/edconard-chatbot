The chart titled "Inflation expectations moving higher" visualizes breakeven inflation rates for one-year and two-year durations from May 2023 to March 2024. 

Key observations:

1. From May 2023 to November 2023, both one-year and two-year breakeven inflation rates mostly fluctuated within the 1.5% to 2.5% range.
2. Following the December FOMC (Federal Open Market Committee) meeting, there is a marked upward trend in both one-year and two-year breakeven inflation rates.
3. By March 2024, the one-year breakeven inflation rate reaches approximately 4.0%, while the two-year breakeven inflation rate rises to around 2.75%.

The chart indicates a significant rise in inflation expectations following the December FOMC meeting, suggesting a market expectation of higher inflation in the near term.
