The bar chart illustrates the net investment inflows into China via the Stock Connect program from 2014 to 2023, measured in billions of Renminbi (Rmb bn).

Key points:
1. The chart shows fluctuations in net investment inflows over the years.
2. Investment inflows peaked in 2021, reaching the highest value observed at over 400 billion RMB.
3. The years 2018 and 2019 saw significant investment inflows, each exceeding 300 billion RMB.
4. A decline started in 2020, with inflows dropping below 300 billion RMB and continuing to decrease in 2022 and into 2023.
5. By 2023, investment inflows are on course for an eight-year low, dropping to levels close to those of 2015.

Sources for this data include FT research and Bloomberg, as of November 20, 2023.
